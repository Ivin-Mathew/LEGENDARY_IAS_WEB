Why Mentorship and Legendary IAS Mentor?

  • Our Mission
  • Our Vision
  • Our Goal
  • Contact

Legendary IAS Mentor is an initiative of nationally reputed civil service Mentors and faculty having high success rate in civil service examinations.Our team consists of subject experts,rank holders, alumni from premier institutions and renowned faculties from Delhi and Hyderabad providing a wide exposure of experience .We use the most modern, state-of-the-art technologies for ensuring effective learning that converges into results. What keeps us apart is the primary importance we give to each of our students. Every single student will be mentored thoroughly, accessing their performance and evaluating their shortcomings, improving on them, thereby instilling in them confidence and the ability to master all subjects. Our vision is to ensure a 100% result for all our students through continued, personalised, and all-inclusive mentorship. Mentorship is aided by classes conducted by renowned faculties who are updated with the current trends of the exam, providing the students with the right knowledge. With the proper mentoring and the right classes that we provide civil service, for you will no longer be "your" dream, it will be "ours," as we will stand with you at all times to make it a reality.

"If you cannot see where you are going, ask someone who has been there before." — J. Loren Norris
The very root of the institution began with the fact that there was a lack of proper mentoring facilities in the country. Clearing the civil service exam is not a strenuous task anymore provided there is a mentor by your side guiding you towards your goal. Here at IAS Mentor, we have grouped together a team of eminent mentors with an unmatched,encompassing and purely novel approach for creating outstanding results.Personal Mentorship holds the crux of our learning philosophy. Each and every aspirant who joins our programme will learn under a personal mentor with whom they can interact at any time. These highly experienced mentors will clear their doubts regarding subjects and exam, create personalized study plans, monitor their daily studies through Q&A sessions, and motivates them. In addition to their academics, our mentors make sure that their psychological health is intact during the course period. We ensure that each aspirant becomes the best.
